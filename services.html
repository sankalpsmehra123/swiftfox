What We Do

Four service lines, one operating standard.

Every SwiftFox deployment — whatever the vertical — runs on the same foundation: trained personnel, structured supervision and clear reporting.

01 / Security Services

Trained protection for every site type

From corporate lobbies to industrial perimeters, our guards are police-verified, uniformed and briefed on site-specific protocols before day one.

  • Armed and unarmed security guards
  • Access control and visitor management
  • Patrolling, perimeter checks and CCTV monitoring support
  • Emergency response and fire-safety readiness
  • Event and corporate security deployments
02 / Housekeeping Solutions

Consistent hygiene, on a fixed schedule

Our housekeeping teams work to written checklists and rotating deep-clean schedules, so standards don't drift between inspections.

  • Daily office and commercial cleaning
  • Deep cleaning and periodic sanitation
  • Floor care, washroom hygiene and waste management
  • Hospital-grade cleaning protocols where required
  • Uniformed, trained housekeeping staff
03 / Facility Management

The operational layer behind the building

Maintenance, utilities and vendor coordination handled by one team, so nothing falls through the cracks between contractors.

  • Building and utility maintenance
  • Front-office and reception support
  • Landscaping and grounds upkeep
  • Third-party vendor coordination
  • Scheduled facility audits and reporting
04 / Manpower Solutions

Staffing that scales with you

Skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled workforce provided on flexible terms — without the overhead of direct hiring, payroll or compliance management.

  • Skilled technical and support staff
  • Semi-skilled operational personnel
  • General unskilled workforce for peak demand
  • Flexible scaling up or down by season or project
  • Full statutory compliance handled on your behalf
Frequently Asked

Common questions about our services

Do you provide services outside Delhi NCR?
Yes — SwiftFox deploys personnel across India on a PAN-India basis. Reach out with your site location and we'll confirm coverage and lead time.
Can we combine multiple services under one contract?
Yes, this is our core model. Most clients combine security, housekeeping and facility management under a single account team rather than managing separate vendors.
How quickly can guards or staff be deployed?
Typical deployment timelines range from a few days to two weeks depending on headcount, role specifications and site verification requirements.
Are your personnel compliant with labour law requirements?
Yes. All deployed personnel are covered under EPF, ESI, Minimum Wages Act and Contract Labour Regulations, with documentation available for client audits.

Not sure which service fits your site?

Tell us a bit about your operations and we'll recommend the right combination of services.
