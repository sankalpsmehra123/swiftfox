About SwiftFox

A single-window partner for facility operations.

SwiftFox Integrated Services brings security, housekeeping, facility management and manpower solutions under one accountable contract, built on trained personnel and structured supervision.

Who We Are

Built for accountability, not just headcount

Most facility vendors specialize in one thing. SwiftFox was built to run security, housekeeping, facility management and manpower under a single account team, so clients deal with one point of contact instead of four separate contracts.

Every deployment follows the same operating standard: trained personnel, documented SOPs, and regular supervision, regardless of which service line it falls under.
